Nicholas Turk

Bio

My name is Nicholas "Nicky" Turk. I'm a 4th year undergraduate student at Georgia Tech with a major in computer science and a minor in linguistics. My concentrations in computer science are intelligence and theory. I've worked as a teaching assistant for two classes at Georgia Tech: CS2050 (Discrete Math for Computer Science) and CS4510 (Automata and Complexity Theory). I've also worked as a research assistant at Montana State University studying the use of stochastic search algorithms for nonlinear regression problems. My main field of research is in computational linguistics, particularly with historical corpora. I'm currently working with the lab of Professor Kartik Goyal to study historical orthography and morphology for Old, Middle, and Early Modern English.
